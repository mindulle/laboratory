reflectedType property
======================

::: {#getter .section .multi-line-signature}
[Type](../../dart-core/type-class) reflectedType

[Type](../../dart-core/type-class). Otherwise, an
[UnsupportedError](../../dart-core/unsupportederror-class) is thrown.
